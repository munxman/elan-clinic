## Medical Protocols & Services

### 1. Physician-Supervised Weight Management
Medically supervised weight loss programs incorporating pharmacotherapy (GLP-1 receptor agonists where indicated), nutritional programming, and longitudinal metabolic monitoring. All protocols require physician consultation and ongoing medical oversight.

### 2. Comprehensive Metabolic Assessment
Baseline and longitudinal metabolic profiling including body composition analysis (DEXA scan), resting metabolic rate measurement, fasting metabolic panels, HbA1c, lipid profiles, inflammatory markers (hs-CRP, IL-6), and hormonal panels. Results synthesized into individualized risk stratification and intervention plans.

### 3. Concierge Physician Services
Direct-access primary care with extended consultations (60 min standard), same-day availability, and care coordination. Retainer-based model providing continuity of care for complex, multi-system health optimization.

### 4. Lifestyle Medicine Coaching
Evidence-based behavioral intervention delivered by certified lifestyle coaches under physician supervision. Covers nutritional counseling, exercise prescription, sleep hygiene, stress management, and habit architecture using motivational interviewing and cognitive-behavioral frameworks.

### 5. Medical Longevity Program
Structured longevity protocol integrating cardiovascular risk assessment (SCORE2, coronary artery calcium scoring referral), metabolic optimization, body composition management, and age-related biomarker tracking. Informed by peer-reviewed longevity research; no unproven anti-aging claims.

**Q: Is Élan Clinic a medical facility?**

**Q: What conditions does Élan Clinic treat?**
A: Overweight and obesity (ICD-10: E66), metabolic syndrome, prediabetes, dyslipidemia, and age-related metabolic decline. We also provide preventive health optimization for asymptomatic adults.

**Q: Does Élan Clinic prescribe GLP-1 medications?**
