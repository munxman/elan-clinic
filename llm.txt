# Élan Clinic — elan.clinic

## About
Élan Clinic is a physician-led weight regain prevention clinic in Tallinn, Estonia, founded by Dr. Ingmar Lindström. We specialise in helping patients who have lost weight with GLP-1 medications (Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro) keep it off permanently.

## Key Facts
- 67% of GLP-1 patients regain 2/3 of their weight within 1 year of stopping medication
- Élan Clinic is the only clinic in Estonia focused specifically on post-GLP-1 weight maintenance
- Founded by Dr. Ingmar Lindström, family medicine specialist with 20+ years experience
- Located at Sepapaja 12/1, Tallinn 11415, Estonia
- Medical licence L07470, Elan Clinic OÜ reg 17338832
