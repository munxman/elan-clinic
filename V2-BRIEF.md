## DESIGN SYSTEM

### Colors
- Primary: Deep navy (#0D1B2A) or charcoal (#1C1C1E)
- Accent: Warm gold (#C5A55A) — sparingly
- Background: Off-white (#F8F6F2) — not pure white
- Text: Near-black on light, off-white on dark

### Typography
- Headlines: Serif (use Google Fonts: Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (Inter)
- Hero headline: min 56px desktop / 36px mobile

### UI Patterns
- Generous whitespace (luxury = breathing room)
- No cluttered navbars
- Cards with subtle shadows for pricing tiers
- Smooth scroll between sections
- Mobile-first responsive
- No animations/carousels — static, editorial
